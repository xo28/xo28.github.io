Research Highlights
Shannon Scaling Law — ICML 2026 (ByteDance Seed)
A unified scaling law that models LLM pretraining as transmission over a noisy channel; reconciles monotonic scaling with U-shaped phenomena (catastrophic overtraining, quantization-induced degradation). Adopted internally at ByteDance Seed for large-scale training-dynamics analysis.
Quantization Scaling Laws + 1500+ Open Checkpoints — ACL 2025 Main (Tencent AI Lab)
Discovered that low-bit quantization favors undertrained LLMs but causes significant degradation on fully-trained models. Released 1500+ quantized LLM checkpoints across model sizes, training-token budgets, and bit widths.
Continuous Diffusion LLMs (ByteDance Applied ML, ongoing)
Adapting a pretrained 16B-parameter MoE LLM into a bidirectional-attention continuous diffusion generator, with a novel manifold-preserving corruption process aligned with the coarse-to-fine semantic structure of language.
